Recent additions to OALD

2020 has been quite a year and as it comes to a close, here are just
a few of the 110+ words and meanings we've added to the Oxford
Learner's Dictionary online:

  • Words related to the pandemic, such as self-isolate,
    contact tracing     and, of course, Covid-19.
  • Words to do with social issues, such as gender-fluid,
    bias and Generation Z.
  • Other words that have become more common, such as
    ultramarathon, plant-based and inspo.
